Main Course:

Fettuccine Al Fredo

Wheat:
24 ounces dry fettuccini pasta

Dairy:
3/4 pint heavy cream
3/4 cup grated Romano cheese
1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
1 cup butter

Spices:
Salt
Pepper

Directions

1)Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil.
2)Add fettuccini and cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until al dente; drain.
3)In a large saucepan, melt butter into cream over low heat. 
4) Add salt, pepper and garlic salt.
5) Stir in cheese over medium heat until melted; this will thicken the sauce.
6)Add pasta to sauce. 
7)Use enough of the pasta so that all of the sauce is used and the pasta is thoroughly coated
8)Serve


Beef Fillet

Dairy:
2 tbsp butter

Spices:
1 tbsp kosher salt
1 tbsp fresh pepper

Meat:
1 fillet of beef

Directions

1)Preheat the oven to 500 degrees F
2)Place the beef on a sheet pan
3)Spread the butter on with your hands
4)Sprinkle evenly with the salt and pepper
5)Roast in the oven for exactly 22 minutes 


Pizza Norma

Wheat:
Pizza dough

Dairy:
Ricotta salata
Mozzarella

Vegetables:
Basil
1 clove garlic
2 Eggplants

Spices:
1 tsp salt
2 tsp olive oil

Sauces:
Marinara

Directions:

1)Stretch the pizza dough to the desired size and thickness and place on a sheet pan.  If you have a baking stone, put the pizza dough on a thin layer of cormeal on the back of a sheet pan to make the transfer easier.  
2)Top with marinara, eggplant mixture, fresh basil and cheeses.  
3)Put the pizza in the oven (or transfer to the baking stone).  
4)Bake until the crust is fully cooked, about 20 minutes unless you have a smokin’ hot oven.
