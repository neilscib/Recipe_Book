DIRECTIONS

1)Cut the mushrooms into slices.
2)Melt butter in large frying pan.
3)Add in onions, garlic, and mushrooms.
4)Cook until onions are soft.
5)Blend in 2 T. flour and stir.
6)Add in the chicken broth and heat until slightly thickened while stirring frequently.
7)Stir cream with additional 1 T. flour and seasonings.
8)Add in cream to soup. Heat to thicken while stirring frequently.
