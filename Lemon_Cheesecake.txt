DESSERT

Lemon Cheesecake

Dairy;
	60g butter 
	300g medium fat cream cheese
	150ml double cream 

Vegetables;
	1 lemon 
	75g caster sugar 

Meat;

Bread;
	200g digestive biscuits

Method;

Crush biscuits 
Add the biscuits to the melted butter and stir until completely mixed
Flatten, and chill in the fridge for around 20 minutes
Squeeze lemon juice and grate zest 
Mix and whisk in the cheese, sugar and double cream
Pour in the lemon juice and zest 
Pour the cream cheese mixture on top of the biscuit base
Put the cake in the fridge to chill for a couple of hours
