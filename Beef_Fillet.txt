MAIN COURSE 

Beef Fillet 

Dairy;
	2 tbsp butter 

Vegetables;
	1 tablespoon kosher salt 
	1 tablespoon fresh pepper 

Meat;
	1 fillet of beef 

Bread;

Method;

Preheat the oven to 500 degrees F
Place the beef on a sheet pan
Spread the butter on with your hands
Sprinkle evenly with the salt and pepper
Roast in the oven for exactly 22 minutes
